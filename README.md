### 👤 About Me

<img align="right" width="38%" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/abhisheknaiidu/abhisheknaiidu/master/code.gif" alt="coding" />

I'm a CS & Business Systems student at SVPCET Nagpur, building toward ML Engineering at FAANG — with a focus most ML engineers don't have: I care as much about whether a model makes business sense as whether it scores well on a benchmark.

My background sits at the deliberate intersection of systems engineering and business reasoning. That shapes how I approach ML — not as a research exercise, but as a production system that must justify its inference cost, scale reliably, and generate measurable impact. A model that can't be trusted in production has no value, regardless of its validation accuracy.

* 🤖 **Applied ML** — building and fine-tuning models for real-world tasks
* ⚙️ **MLOps Focus** — model serving, deployment pipelines, and observability
* 📊 **Data-First Thinking** — clean data and sound statistics before complex models
* 💡 **Business-Aware Systems** — designing ML with cost, scale, and ROI in mind

<br/>

**Open To:** AI/ML Internships · Research Collaborations · Open Source Contributions · Hackathon Teams
